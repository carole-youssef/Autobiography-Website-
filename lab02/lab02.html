My journey through Egypt was an incredible experience filled with beautiful sights, delicious food, and amazing people. Join me as I share some of the natural wonders I visited, as well as some famous Egyptian dishes.

Natural Wonders I Visited

The Nile River

  • The Nile is the longest river in the world, with a length of 6,650 km
  • A lot of ancient sites, like Luxor and Karnak, are found along its banks

Mount Sinai

  • Mount Sinai is notably known as the location where Moses received the Ten Commandments.
  • Many historical places are found there, such as St. Catherine's Monastery.

The Red Sea

  • The Red Sea is known for its clear waters and dazzilng coral reefs.
  • It is a top destination for sailing, diving, kite surfing and snorkeling.

Famous Cultural Dishes

Koshari

A famous Egyptian dish made with rice, lentils, and macaroni, topped with tomato sauce and crispy onions.

Falafel

These are deep-fried balls made from ground chickpeas and are usually served in pita bread.

Molokhia

An Egyptian dish made from diced jute leaves cooked in a chicken broth, usually topped with garlic and coriander and paired with rice or Egyptian bread.

Hawawshi

A famous Egyptian street food involving seasoned ground meat, generally lamb or beef, packed into Egyptian bread and then baked or grilled.
